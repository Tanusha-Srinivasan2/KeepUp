# KeepUp Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how KeepUp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Account registration information (email, username)
- Profile preferences and settings
- Quiz answers and learning progress
- Feedback and support communications

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- App usage data (features used, time spent)
- Performance and crash reports

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Precise location data
- Contact lists or phone numbers
- Personal photos or media files
- Financial or payment information (unless making purchases)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the KeepUp service
- Personalize your news and quiz experience
- Track your learning progress and achievements
- Communicate important updates and notifications
- Ensure safety and prevent abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Children's Privacy

### 3.1 Age Restrictions
KeepUp is designed for users aged **13 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 3.2 Parental Notice
If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@keepup.app. We will promptly delete such information.

### 3.3 COPPA Compliance
We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Third parties that help us operate the App (hosting, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect safety
- **Safety Reporting**: Child safety violations will be reported to NCMEC and law enforcement

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure data storage practices

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@keepup.app.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

These services have their own privacy policies governing their data practices.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
